# Placeholder guide (project + template)

This repo uses placeholder tokens in some operational documents (especially anything copied from the upstream template).

## Common placeholders

- `{{APP_NAME}}`: MileTrack
- `{{BUNDLE_ID}}`: your bundle identifier (example: `com.company.miletrack`)
- `{{DOMAIN}}`: your website domain (example: `miletrack.app`)
- `{{SUPPORT_EMAIL}}`: support email
- `{{PRIVACY_EMAIL}}`: privacy email

## Where placeholders appear

- `legal/`
- `release/`
- `support_site/`
- `_template_upstream/` (if you ran `./scripts/template_sync.sh`)

## Replace placeholders

Search for `{{` and replace tokens with real values.
