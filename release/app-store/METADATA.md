## Description

Stop logging trips manually. MileTrack tracks every mile automatically so your tax deduction happens in the background while you drive.

Set it and forget it.
MileTrack detects when you start driving and logs your trip automatically. No tapping. No forgetting. Just open the app at tax time and your mileage is already there.

A smarter inbox for your trips.
Every detected trip lands in your Inbox. Swipe right to accept, swipe left to dismiss. It takes seconds to classify a week of driving. Merge short trips into one. Add a purpose. Done.

Built for real tax deductions.
The IRS requires a written record of business mileage - date, destination, business purpose, and miles. MileTrack captures all of it. Export a clean PDF or CSV whenever your accountant asks.

Know your deduction in real time.
The dashboard shows your total deductible miles and estimated tax savings using the current IRS standard mileage rate - updated automatically each year.

Works across all your devices.
iCloud sync keeps your trips consistent on every iPhone.

Who uses MileTrack:
- Freelancers and consultants driving to client sites
- Gig workers (Uber, DoorDash, Instacart) tracking delivery miles
- Real estate agents, photographers, contractors
- Anyone who drives for work and wants the deduction at tax time

Key features:
- Automatic trip detection - starts and stops without you
- Inbox with swipe-to-accept UX
- Trip merging and manual entry
- Business purpose tagging
- Real-time deduction dashboard
- PDF and CSV export (IRS-ready)
- iCloud sync across devices
- Charts and mileage analytics
- Clean, minimal interface

MileTrack Premium unlocks unlimited trip history, advanced reporting, and priority export. Try it free.
