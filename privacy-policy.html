Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 28, 2026

MileTrack by Plenitudo ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the MileTrack iOS application (the "App").

Key Point: MileTrack is a privacy-first app. All your data stays on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no ads. We never see your trip data.

1. Information the App Stores

MileTrack stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Trip data: Date, distance, duration, start/end locations (GPS coordinates and addresses), category, purpose, client/organization, vehicle, and notes
  • Saved locations: Named places you create (e.g., "Home", "Office") with addresses and coordinates
  • Settings: Your preferences, categories, clients, vehicles, mileage rates, and auto-categorization rules
  • Receipts: Expense receipt metadata and images you attach to trips
  • Diagnostics: An on-device log of tracking events (no location names or PII) for troubleshooting

2. iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud Drive enabled, MileTrack backs up your trip data to your private iCloud container. This data is:

  • Encrypted by Apple in transit and at rest on Apple's servers
  • Stored in your personal iCloud account — we cannot access it
  • Used solely to restore your trips if you reinstall the app or switch devices

You can disable iCloud Drive for MileTrack in your device's Settings > Apple ID > iCloud.

3. Location Data

MileTrack uses your device's GPS to automatically detect and track trips. Location data is:

  • Processed entirely on your device
  • Never sent to our servers (we have no servers)
  • Used only to calculate trip distance and determine start/end addresses via Apple's built-in geocoding service
  • Stored as part of your trip records on your device

You can revoke location permission at any time in Settings > Privacy > Location Services > MileTrack.

4. Motion Data

MileTrack uses your device's motion sensors (accelerometer) to help distinguish driving from other activities. This data is processed in real-time on your device and is not stored or transmitted.

5. Subscriptions

MileTrack offers optional Pro subscriptions managed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. All billing is handled by Apple. See Apple's privacy policy for details on how Apple handles purchase data.

6. What We Do NOT Collect

MileTrack does not:

  • Create user accounts or require login
  • Collect email addresses, names, or personal identifiers
  • Use analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising SDKs
  • Share, sell, or transmit your data to any third party
  • Use cookies or web tracking technologies
  • Send marketing emails or push notifications
  • Contain any third-party code or SDKs

7. Data Export and Deletion

  • Export: You can export all your trip data as CSV or PDF from the Reports tab at any time
  • Delete: You can delete individual trips, or delete all data from Settings > Privacy & Data > Delete All Data
  • Uninstall: Deleting the app removes all local data. iCloud backup data can be removed via Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Storage

8. Data Security

Your data is protected by:

  • iOS file protection (NSFileProtectionComplete) — data is encrypted when your device is locked
  • App sandbox isolation — no other app can access MileTrack's data
  • iCloud encryption for synced data (Apple-managed)

9. Children's Privacy

MileTrack is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
